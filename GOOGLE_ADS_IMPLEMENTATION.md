# Google Ads Integration Guide for WorkbridgeJobs

## Overview
This guide covers implementing Google AdSense on the WorkbridgeJobs platform to monetize traffic while maintaining excellent user experience.

## 1. Google AdSense Setup Process

### Step 1: Apply for Google AdSense
1. Visit [Google AdSense](https://www.google.com/adsense/)
2. Sign up with your Google account
3. Add your website URL: `https://your-domain.com`
4. Select your country/territory
5. Choose payment currency
6. Review and accept AdSense Terms & Conditions

### Step 2: Website Requirements Checklist
- [x] Original, high-quality content
- [x] Clear navigation structure
- [x] Privacy Policy (create one)
- [x] Terms of Service (create one)
- [x] Contact information
- [x] Mobile-responsive design
- [x] Fast loading times
- [x] HTTPS enabled (required)

### Step 3: Site Verification
Google will review your site for:
- Content quality and originality
- Navigation and user experience
- Compliance with AdSense policies
- Traffic volume (some countries require minimum traffic)

## 2. Technical Implementation

### Ad Placement Strategy for Job Boards
1. **Header Banner** - Above navigation (728x90 leaderboard)
2. **Sidebar Ads** - Next to job listings (300x250 medium rectangle)
3. **In-content Ads** - Between job cards (responsive)
4. **Footer Ads** - Bottom of pages (728x90 leaderboard)
5. **Mobile Sticky** - Bottom of mobile screens (320x50)

### Performance Considerations
- Lazy load ads to improve page speed
- Limit ads to maintain user experience
- Use responsive ad units
- Monitor Core Web Vitals impact

## 3. AdSense Policy Compliance

### Content Requirements
- ✅ Job listings are valuable content
- ✅ No prohibited content (adult, violence, etc.)
- ✅ Original descriptions and company information
- ✅ Regular content updates

### User Experience Requirements
- Ads should not interfere with navigation
- Clear distinction between ads and content
- No accidental clicks encouraged
- Mobile-friendly ad placement

## 4. Revenue Optimization Tips

### Ad Placement Best Practices
1. **Above the fold** - At least one ad visible without scrolling
2. **Near content** - Close to job listings for relevance
3. **Multiple sizes** - Use various ad formats
4. **A/B testing** - Test different placements

### Expected Revenue (Estimates)
- **Job boards typically earn**: $0.50-$3.00 per 1000 page views
- **South African traffic**: $0.20-$1.50 RPM average
- **Government job searches**: Higher CPC due to competition

## 5. Legal Requirements
